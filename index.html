Ranchi’s leading store for musical instruments for more than 30 years. Led and nurtured by the scion of a prominent musical family providing music related services in different cities of the Chhotanagpur region, New Chandan Musicals deals in all types of musical instruments, music books, printed sheet music, for-hire stage mikes, DJ systems and a wide range of music accessories. You will find leading brands in guitars, flutes, sitar, tanpura, thalam, pianos, keyboards, drums, harmonicas and more, besides their complete care and servicing. Top brands like Yamaha, Korg, Gibson, Fender, Mapex, Pearl Drums, Takamine, Zildjian, Taylor Guitars, D'Addario Strings and many more are all available in-store. Regularly updated news, concert listings and music events keeps you abreast with the latest in the music world. The entire experience revolves around making choice of an instrument more interactive and engaging.
